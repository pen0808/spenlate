## About Spenlate

**Spenlate** is an advanced, AI-powered speech-to-text and real-time translation tool designed to bridge the gap between spoken words and written text across global languages. 

The name itself reflects its core DNA: **Sp**eech + **Pen** + Trans**late**. It acts as a digital pen that effortlessly keeps pace with natural human speech, transforming live audio or recorded voice files into highly accurate, structured text—and instantly translating it into your target language.

### Key Features

*   **AI-Driven Speech-to-Text:** Converts complex audio into precise text with high context awareness, easily handling accents, technical jargon, and natural pauses.
*   **Instant Translation:** Breaks down language barriers on the fly, allowing you to translate spoken audio into multiple languages seamlessly.
*   **Sleek & Context-Aware:** Built with modern AI models to ensure that translations maintain their original meaning, tone, and cultural nuance.
